How to play

The aim of the game is to guess a number generated randomly between 1 and 100. After each guess, you are warned if your guess is higher or lower than the generated number. You only have 10 tries, after that the game is over. You mustn't enter alphabets, special characters (e.g. "%", "#", etc.), 0 or negative numbers and numbers over 100. Good luck!
